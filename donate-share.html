Campaign support plan

A $50,000 goal focused on safety, stability, and basic needs.

This campaign would raise money to support emergency housing, counseling, life skills classes, transportation, clothing, food, school supplies, and other resources for youth and young adults served by Project Oz.

Why $50,000?

Because crisis support needs more than awareness.

The campaign goal gives the community a clear target to rally around. Money raised would help Project Oz continue providing practical support for youth facing homelessness, family conflict, mental health challenges, and other crisis situations.

Visit Project Oz donation page

Basic needs

Food, clothing, hygiene items, school supplies, transportation support, and other essentials.

Stability services

Emergency housing, transitional housing support, counseling, outreach, and life skills resources.

Share the campaign

Turn one scan into more visibility.

Flyers and social media posts should point people back to the campaign website. The message should stay simple: Project Oz supports local youth in crisis, the campaign has a $50,000 goal, and the community can help by donating, sharing, or learning more.

  • Post the QR code on campaign flyers.
  • Use short captions that explain the local need.
  • Encourage students and community members to share the link.

Past community support

Project Oz has used community fundraising events, anniversary fundraising efforts, local business partnerships, an Amazon Wish List, supply drives, and online donations to support its work.

How people can help

Community members can donate online, share the campaign, contribute needed supplies, support local fundraising events, or learn about Project Oz volunteer and Host Home opportunities.
