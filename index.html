Campaign overview

This proposed campaign uses a simple QR code placement to turn everyday local food orders into a quick awareness opportunity. When someone scans the code, they are directed to this site to learn about Project Oz and the services it provides for youth and young adults in crisis.

01

Raise awareness

Introduce local students, families, and community members to Project Oz and the problems it helps address.

02

Support a $50,000 goal

The campaign goal would help support emergency housing, counseling, life skills classes, transportation, school supplies, food, clothing, and other basic needs.

03

Make support easy

The QR code keeps the action simple: scan, learn, share, and support Project Oz’s work with local youth.

Why DP Dough boxes?

A practical local touchpoint

DP Dough is close to the Illinois State University campus, where many young adults live, eat, study, and spend time with friends. A QR code on the box meets people in a low-effort moment when they already have their phone nearby.
